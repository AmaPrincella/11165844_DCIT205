About Us

Our Mission

The College of Basic and Applied Sciences includes the computer science department. For those who wish to study computer science, we have programs available. Postgraduate and undergraduate courses are offered by the department. In computer science, undergraduate students can obtain a Bachelor of Science (BSc) degree, while graduate students can pursue degrees in Master of Philosophy (MPhil) and Doctor of Philosophy (PhD). The department's primary goals are to teach and do research in a variety of computer science fields, including cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, programming, and algorithms. The instructors are experts in their disciplines with a wealth of information. They support students' learning and research endeavors. To help students with their studies, the department offers a library, computer labs, and other services.
We offer a world-class research facility combining theory with practical activities, and significantly advancing both the field and computing in the wider world. The Department sustains active research across the breadth of computer science and encourages the development of new technologies and applications.

Key Information

Our programs are created to address the dearth of graduates with advanced degrees in the computer industry, which offers great job opportunities. The Department of Computer Science, which accepts students from all over the world, is fulfilling an important responsibility by producing computer science professionals qualified to meet the global needs for industry and research, while also keeping in mind that university departments have an obligation to develop skilled and competent professionals to face global challenges with new insights of problem-solving abilities. The department's goal of turning out capable and competent computer science professionals is being met thanks to the hard work and dedication of its highly qualified academic members.
